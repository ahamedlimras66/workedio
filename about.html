About Us

Nowadays, it's hard to find a good job in this competitive world. We have to prepare well for that interview, but students don't know how to prepare or where to apply for a job. To overcome that problem, we decided to create Workedio. This website will help you prepare for the aptitude, reasoning, verbal test, and technical rounds. Additionally, we have the Workedio bot to train you for the HR interview round, and we have developed a free resume builder to create resumes for fresher and experienced individuals. Furthermore, we have a job searching portal to easily apply for jobs.
